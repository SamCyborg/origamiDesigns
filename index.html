ORIGAMI CAMEL

Interesting Facts About Camels

  1. Camels are very strong and can carry up to 900 pounds for 25 miles a day.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Their humps let them store up to 80 pounds of fat which they can live off for weeks and even months!

ORIGAMI CHAMELEON

Interesting Facts About Chameleons

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are a part of iguana suborder
  2. Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons
  3. Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they used to wrap around tree branches

ORIGAMI CICADA

Interesting Facts About Cicadas

  1. Most cicadas have red-orange eyes. But occasionally cicadas have blue, white or grayish eyes
  2. Apart from being loud – their sound is a cross between a buzz and a rattle
  3. Cicadas have two pairs of membranous wings, prominent compound eyes, and three simple eyes (ocelli)
